Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-152, the “Fair Criminal Record Screening Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-642. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 3, 2014, and July 14, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Aug. 21, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-422 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-152 became effective on Dec. 17, 2014.
