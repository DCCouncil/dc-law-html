Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 18-161, the “Third & H Streets, N.E. Economic Development Act of 2010”, was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 18-432, which was referred to the Committee on Finance and Revenue. The bill was adopted on first and second readings on March 2, 2010, and March 16, 2010, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on April 2, 2010, it was assigned Act No. 18-353 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 18-161 became effective on May 27, 2010.
