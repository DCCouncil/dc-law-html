Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-34, the “Private Contractor and Subcontractor Prompt Payment Act of 2013,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-145. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 26, 2013, and July 10, 2013, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on August 2, 2013, it was assigned Act No. 20-148 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-34 became effective on November 5, 2013.
