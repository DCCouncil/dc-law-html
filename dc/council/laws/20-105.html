Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-105, the “Senior Citizen Real Property Tax Relief Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-318. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on January 7, 2014, and March 4, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on March 25, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-303 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-105 became effective on May 28, 2014.
