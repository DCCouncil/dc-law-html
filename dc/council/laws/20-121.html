Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-121, the “Breastmilk Bank and Lactation Support Act of 2013,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-410. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on April 8, 2014, and May 6, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on May 28, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-340 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-121 became effective on July 15, 2014.
