Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 14-232, the “Mandarin Oriental Hotel Project Tax Deferral Act of 2002”, was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 14-466, which was referred to the Committee on Finance and Revenue. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on July 2, 2002, and October 1, 2002, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on October 23, 2002, it was assigned Act No. 14-489 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 14-232 became effective on March 25, 2003.
