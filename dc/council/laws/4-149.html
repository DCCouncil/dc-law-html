Legislative History
Law 4-149 was, the “Drug Paraphernalia Act of 1982,” introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 4-5, which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on May 25, 1982, and June 22, 1982, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on July 21, 1982, it was assigned Act No. 4-220 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
