Legislative History
Law 7-68, “District of Columbia Public School Support Initiative of 1986,” was submitted to the electors of the District of Columbia on November 3, 1987, as Initiative No. 25. The results of the voting, certified by the Board of Elections and Ethics on November 17, 1987, were 54,729 for the Initiative and 16,223 against the Initiative. It was assigned Act No. 7-102 after certification and was transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review on November 23, 1987.
