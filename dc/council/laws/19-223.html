Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 19-223, the “Employee Transportation Amendment Act of 2012,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 19-354. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on July 10, 2012, and Oct. 2, 2012, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Nov. 5, 2012, it was assigned Act No. 19-534 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 19-223 became effective on Mar. 5, 2013.
