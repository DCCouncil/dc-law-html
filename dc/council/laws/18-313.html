Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 18-313, the “District of Columbia Uniform Law Commission Act of 2010”, was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 18-579, which was referred to the Committee on Public Safety and the Judiciary. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on November 9, 2010, and November 23, 2010, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on December 9, 2010, it was assigned Act No. 18-634 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 18-313 became effective on March 12, 2011.
