Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 19-206, the “Pedestrian and Bicyclist Protection Amendment Act of 2012,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 19-568. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 26, 2012, and Oct. 2, 2012, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Oct. 23, 2012, it was assigned Act No. 19-486 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 19-206 became effective on Mar. 5, 2013.
