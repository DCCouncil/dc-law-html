Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 18-233, the “Corrections Information Council Amendment Act of 2010”, was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 18-404, which was referred to the Committee on Public Safety and the Judiciary. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on April 20, 2010, and May 4, 2010, respectively. Became law without signature of the Mayor on May 22, 2010, it was assigned Act No. 18-406 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 18-233 became effective on October 2, 2010.
