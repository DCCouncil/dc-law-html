Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-191, the “Food Policy Council and Director Establishment Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-821. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Oct. 7, 2014, and Oct. 28, 2014, respectively. Returned without the Mayor’s signature on Nov. 19, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-483 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-191 became effective on Mar. 10, 2015.
