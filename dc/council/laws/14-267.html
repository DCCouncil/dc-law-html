Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 14-267, the “Vacant and Abandoned Properties Community Development and Disapproval of Disposition of Certain Scattered Vacant and Abandon Properties Act of 2002”, was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 14-675, which was referred to the Committee on Economic Development. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on December 3, 2002, and December 17, 2002, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on January 7, 2003, it was assigned Act No. 14-579 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 14-267 became effective on April 2, 2003.
