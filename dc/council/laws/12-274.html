Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 12-274, the “Health Benefits Plan Members Bill of Rights Act of 1998,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 12-501. The Bill was adopted on first and second reading on December 1, 1998, and December 15, 1998, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on December 29, 1998, it was assigned Act No. 12-607, and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for review. D.C. Law 12-274 became effective on April 27, 1999.
