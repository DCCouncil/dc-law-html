Legislative History
Law 1-130, the “Resocialization Furlough Act of 1976,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 1-223, which was referred to the Committee on Public Safety. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on July 20, 1976 and November 23, 1976, respectively. Enacted without signature by the Mayor on January 19, 1977, it was assigned Act No. 1-224 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
