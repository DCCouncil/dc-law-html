Legislative History
Law 1-95 was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 1-323, which was referred to the Committee on Employment and Economic Development. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on September 15, 1976 and October 12, 1976, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on November 15, 1976, it was assigned Act No. 1-174 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
