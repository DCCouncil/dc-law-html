Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-135, the “Air Quality Amendment Act of 2013,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-368. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on May 6, 2014, and June 3, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on June 23, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-365 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-135 became effective on Sep. 9, 2014.
