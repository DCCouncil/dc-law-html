Legislative History
Law 1-123, the “Retail Service Station Act of 1976.” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 1-333, which was referred to the Committee on Transportation and Environmental Affairs. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on November 23, 1976 and December 7, 1976, respectively. Enacted without signature by the Mayor on January 18, 1977, it was assigned Act No. 1-220 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
