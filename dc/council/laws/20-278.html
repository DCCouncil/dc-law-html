Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-278, the “Civil Asset Forfeiture Amendment Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-48. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on November 18, 2014, and December 2, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on February 5, 2015, it was assigned Act No. 20-619 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-278 became effective June 16, 2015.
