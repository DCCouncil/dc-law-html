Legislative History
Law 4-158, “District of Columbia Board of Education Leasing Authority Act of 1982,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 4-223, which was referred to the Committee on Government Operations. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 22, 1982, and July 6, 1982, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on July 29, 1982, it was assigned Act No. 4-232 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review.
