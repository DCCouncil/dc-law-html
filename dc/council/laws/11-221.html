Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 11-221, the “Lead-Based Paint Abatement and Control Act of 1996,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 11-640, which was referred to the Committee on Housing and Urban Affairs. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on October 1, 1996, and November 17, 1996, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on November 20, 1996, it was assigned Act No. 11-438 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 11-221 became effective April 9, 1997.
