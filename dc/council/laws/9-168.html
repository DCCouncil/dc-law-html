Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 9-168 was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 9-418, which was referred to the Committee on Human Services. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 2, 1992, and July 7, 1992, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on July 23, 1992, it was assigned Act No. 9-266 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 9-168 became effective on October 1, 1992.
