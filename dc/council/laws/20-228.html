Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-228, the “Public-Private Partnership Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-595. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Oct. 28, 2014, and Dec. 2, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Dec. 29, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-550 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-228 became effective on Mar. 11, 2015.
