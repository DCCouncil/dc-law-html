Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 13-297, the “Nurse’s Rehabilitation Program Act of 2001”, was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 13-794, which was referred to the Committee on Human Services. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on December 5, 2000, and December 19, 2001, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on January 24, 2001, it was assigned Act No. 13-587 and transmitted to Both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 13-297 became effective on May 1, 2001.
