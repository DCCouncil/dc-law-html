Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-110, the “Skyland Town Center Omnibus Act of 2013,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-382. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Mar. 4, 2014, and April 8, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on April 28, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-309 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-110 became effective on June 21, 2014.
