Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 21-14, the “Prohibition of Pre-Employment Marijuana Testing Act of 2015” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 21-25. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on April 14, 2015, and May 5, 2015, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on May 22, 2015, it was assigned Act No. 21-67 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 21-14 became effective July 22, 2015.
