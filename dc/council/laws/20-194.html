Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-194, the “Special Education Student Rights Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-723. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Oct. 7, 2014, and Oct. 28, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Nov. 20, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-486 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-194 became effective on Mar. 10, 2015.
