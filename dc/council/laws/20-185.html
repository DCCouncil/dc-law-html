Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 20-185, the “Stroke System of Care Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-327. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Oct. 7, 2014, and Oct. 28, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Nov. 12, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-469 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-185 became effective on Mar. 10, 2015.
