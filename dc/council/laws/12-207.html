Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 12-207, the “Uniform Per Student Funding Formula for Public Schools and Public Charter Schools and Tax Conformity Clarification Amendment Act of 1998,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 12-615, which was referred to the Committee on Education, Libraries, and Recreation. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on July 7, 1998, and September 22, 1998, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on October 16, 1998, it was assigned Act No. 12-494 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 12-207 became effective on March 26, 1999.
