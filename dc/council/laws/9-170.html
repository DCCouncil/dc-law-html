Legislative History (LIMS)
Law 9-170, the “Medicare Supplement Insurance Minimum Standards Act of 1992,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 9-459, which was referred to the Committee on Human Services and reassigned to the Committee on Consumer and Regulatory Affairs. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 2, 1992, and July 7, 1992, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on July 23, 1992, it was assigned Act No. 9-268 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 9-170 became effective on October 1, 1992.
