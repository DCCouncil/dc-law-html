Subchapter XIV. Transition Provisions.

§ 29–314.01. Application to existing domestic corporations. § 29–314.02. Application to registered foreign corporations.

§ 29–314.01. Application to existing domestic corporations.

Except as otherwise provided by § 29-107.01, this chapter shall apply to all domestic corporations in existence on its effective date that were incorporated under any general statute of the District providing for incorporation of corporations for profit.


(July 2, 2011, D.C. Law 18-378, § 2, 58 DCR 1720; Mar. 5, 2013, D.C. Law 19-210, § 2(c)(36), 59 DCR 13171.)

Effect of Amendments

The 2013 amendment by D.C. Law 19-210 added “Except as otherwise provided by § 29-107.01.”

Editor's Notes

Application of Law 19-210: Section 7 of D.C. Law 19-210 provided that the act shall apply as of January 1, 2012.

§ 29–314.02. Application to registered foreign corporations.

A foreign corporation authorized to do business in the District on the effective date of this chapter shall be subject to this chapter but is not required to obtain a new certificate of registration to do business under this chapter.


(July 2, 2011, D.C. Law 18-378, § 2, 58 DCR 1720; Mar. 5, 2013, D.C. Law 19-210, § 2(c)(37), 59 DCR 13171.)

Effect of Amendments

The 2013 amendment by D.C. Law 19-210 substituted “registered” for “qualified” in the section heading.

Editor's Notes

Application of Law 19-210: Section 7 of D.C. Law 19-210 provided that the act shall apply as of January 1, 2012.
