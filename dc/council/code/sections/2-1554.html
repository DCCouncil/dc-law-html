§ 2–1554. Biannual assessment and funding report.

The Children and Youth Investment Corporation, or a successor single non-service provider, nonprofit organization, shall submit a biannual assessment and funding report to the Council, which includes:

(1) A research-based needs assessment of at-risk youth, which identifies:

(A) Available resources;

(B) Needed resources, if any; and

(C) Any gap in services; and

(2) A list of funding priorities based upon the needs assessment.


(Oct. 20, 1999, D.C. Law 13-38, § 2404a; as added Mar. 3, 2010, D.C. Law 18-111, § 5061(b), 57 DCR 181; Apr. 7, 2017, D.C. Law 21-261, § 10, 64 DCR 2090.)

Applicability

Applicability of D.C. Law 21-261: § 12 of D.C. Law 21-261 provided that the repeal of this section by § 10 of D.C. Law 21-261 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been given effect.
