§ 31–2993.03. Right to file grievance.

This chapter shall not limit, prohibit, or modify a qualified individual’s right to:

(1) File a grievance and use an independent review process, if available; or

(2) Use the independent medical review system.


(June 5, 2008, D.C. Law 17-166, § 4, 55 DCR 5174.)
