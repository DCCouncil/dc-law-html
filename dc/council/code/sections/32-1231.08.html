§ 32–1231.08. Judicial review.

(a) A party contesting a determination of the Commission on Human Rights shall have the right to judicial review as provided by § 2-1403.14.

(b) A party contesting a determination of an independent hearing examiner shall have the right to judicial review as provided by 4 DCMR § 121.


(Mar. 3, 2015, D.C. Law 20-168, § 9, 61 DCR 11368; Dec. 13, 2017, D.C. Law 22-33, § 2282(e), 64 DCR 7652.)
