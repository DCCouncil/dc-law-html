§ 4–753.07. Local rent supplement program referrals.

Notwithstanding § 4-753.05, during Fiscal Year 2015, the District of Columbia Housing Authority shall fill 75 tenant-based Rent Supplement Program vouchers, established by § 6-228, through referrals by the Department of Human Services. The referrals shall be based on special eligibility criteria established in 29 DCMR § 2557.1 , or shall consist of families currently in the Department of Human Services Permanent Supportive Housing Program who the Department determines no longer need intensive services.


(Oct. 22, 2005, D.C. Law 16-35, § 8d [8e]; as added Feb. 26, 2015, D.C. Law 20-155, § 5162, 61 DCR 9990; renumbered 8e.)
