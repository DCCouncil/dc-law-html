§ 32–332. Rules.

 

The Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, shall issue rules to implement the provisions of this subchapter.


(Apr. 12, 2005, D.C. Law 15-332, § 3, 52 DCR 1989.)
