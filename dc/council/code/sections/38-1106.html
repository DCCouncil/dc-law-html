§ 38–1106. Appropriations.

 

There is authorized to be appropriated from the revenues of the District of Columbia an amount not to exceed $50,000,000 to carry out the purposes of this subchapter and subchapter II of this chapter. The authorization made by this section shall include any amounts made available pursuant to § 10-619.


(Nov. 7, 1966, 80 Stat. 1433, Pub. L. 89-791, title III, § 301(a).)
