§ 48–715. Exceptions.

 

This chapter shall not apply to any cosmetic unless the cosmetic is a drug as defined by § 201 of the Food, Drug and Cosmetic Act [21 U.S.C. § 321 ].


(June 13, 1990, D.C. Law 8-137, § 16, 37 DCR 2631.)

Prior Codifications

1981 Ed., § 33-1015.

References in Text

Section 201 of the Food, Drug and Cosmetic Act is codified at 21 U.S.C. § 321.

Editor's Notes

The reference to “§ 201 of the Food, Drug and Cosmetic Act” was corrected from “§ 321 of the Food, Drug and Cosmetic Act” as it appeared in D.C. Law 8-137.
