§ 38–2913. Services.

Beginning in Fiscal Year 2020, services provided by District of Columbia government agencies to public schools shall be provided on an equitable basis to the District of Columbia Public Schools and public charter schools. Any services that are funded apart from the Uniform per Student Funding Formula shall not also be funded by the Uniform Per Student Funding Formula.


(Mar. 26, 1999, D.C. Law 12-207, § 115; as added Sept. 24, 2010, D.C. Law 18-223, § 4062, 57 DCR 6242; Apr. 8, 2011, D.C. Law 18-370, § 402(d), 58 DCR 1008; Sept. 20, 2012, D.C. Law 19-168, § 4072, 59 DCR 8025; Dec. 24, 2013, D.C. Law 20-61, § 4102, 60 DCR 12472; Feb. 26, 2015, D.C. Law 20-155, § 4062, 61 DCR 9990; Oct. 22, 2015, D.C. Law 21-36, § 4002(c), 62 DCR 10905; Oct. 8, 2016, D.C. Law 21-160, § 4002(d), 63 DCR 10775.)
