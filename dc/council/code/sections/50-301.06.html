§ 50–301.06. District of Columbia Taxicab Commission — Organization.

 
The Commission may organize task-specific panels as its needs dictate. All acts and orders issued by a panel shall be ratified by a majority of the appointed members of the Commission before taking effect.
(Mar. 25, 1986, D.C. Law 6-97, § 7, 33 DCR 703; May 10, 1988, D.C. Law 7-109, § 2(a), 35 DCR 2181; Apr. 9, 1997, D.C. Law 11-198, § 501(b), 43 DCR 4569; Oct. 22, 2012, D.C. Law 19-184, § 2(f), 59 DCR 9431.)
