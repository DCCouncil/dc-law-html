§ 9–251. Definitions.

For the purposes of this chapter, the terms "commemorative work" and "public space" shall have the same meanings as provided for in § 9-204.11.


(Mar. 16, 2021, D.C. Law 23-197, § 2, 68 DCR 000757.)
