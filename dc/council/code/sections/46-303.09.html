§ 46–303.09. Private counsel.

 
An individual may employ private counsel to represent the individual in proceedings authorized by this chapter.
(Feb. 9, 1996, D.C. Law 11-81, § 308, 42 DCR 6748; redesignated § 309, June 22, 2006, D.C. Law 16-137, § 2(c)(9), 53 DCR 3634.)
Prior Codifications
1981 Ed., § 30-343.8.
2001 Ed., § 46-303.08
Effective Dates
Applicability: Section 3 of D.C. Law 16-137 provided: “This act shall apply as of April 1, 2007.”
Editor's Notes
Former § 46-303.09 has been recodified as § 46-303.10 by D.C. Law 16-137, § 2(c)(10).
Uniform Law: This section is based upon § 309 of the Uniform Interstate Family Support Act (2001 Act).
