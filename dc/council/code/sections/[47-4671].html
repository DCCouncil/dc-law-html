§ [47-4671]. Mypheduh Films DBA Sankofa Video and Books, Lot 884, Square 2885. [Not Funded]

Not Funded.


(Sept. 11, 2019, D.C. Law 23-24, § 2, 66 DCR 9759.)

Applicability

Applicability of D.C. Law 23-24: § 4 of D.C. Law 23-24 provided that the creation of this section by § 2 of D.C. Law 23-24 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been implemented.

Emergency Legislation

For temporary (90 days) creation of § 47-4672, see § 2 of MLK Gateway Real Property Tax Abatement Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2019 (D.C. Act 23-139, Oct. 23, 2019, 66 DCR 14318).

Temporary Legislation

For temporary (225 days) creation of § 47-4672, see § 2(b) of MLK Gateway Real Property Tax Abatement Temporary Amendment Act of 2019 (D.C. Law 23-28, Nov. 26, 2019, 66 DCR 13120).
