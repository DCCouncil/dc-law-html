§ 36–641.12. Game of skill machine use by minors prohibited.

*NOTE: This section was created by emergency legislation that will expire on December 30, 2020.*

(a) A licensee shall not permit a person under the age of 18 to use or play a game of skill machine.

(b) The Office may suspend or revoke a license and issue a fine, in accordance with § 36-641.15, against a licensee that knowingly allows a person under the age of 18 to use or play a game of skill machine.


(Mar. 10, 1981, D.C. Law 3-172, § 412; as added Oct. 1, 2020, D.C. Act 23-479, § 2(b), 67 DCR 13284.)
