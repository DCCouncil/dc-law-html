§ 7–751.16. Rulemaking.

The Mayor is authorized to promulgate rules necessary to implement this chapter.


(Mar. 8, 2006, D.C. Law 16-71, § 17, 53 DCR 61.)

Delegation of Authority

Delegation of Authority pursuant to DC Law 16-71, the District of Columbia Health Professional Recruitment Program Act of 2005, see Mayor’s Order 2009-176, October 13, 2009 ( 56 DCR 8480).
