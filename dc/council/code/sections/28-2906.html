§ 28–2906. Nonliability of corporation and transfer agent.

A corporation or transfer agent does not incur liability to any person by making a transfer or otherwise acting in a manner authorized by this chapter.


(Aug. 30, 1964, 78 Stat. 674, Pub. L. 88-509, § 1.)
