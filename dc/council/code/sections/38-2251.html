§ 38–2251. Definitions.

*NOTE: This section was created by emergency legislation that will expire on November 21, 2021.*

For purposes of this subtitle, the term:

(1) "Child development facility" shall have the same meaning as provided in § 7-2031(3).

(2) "Community-based organization" or "CBO" shall have the same meaning as provided in § 38-271.01(1C).

(3) "Early childhood development provider" shall have the same meaning as provided in § 38-271.01(1G).

(4) "Subsidy" means supplemental payments made by the Mayor pursuant to § 4-404.01.


(Aug. 23, 2021, D.C. Act 24-159, § 1102, 0 DCR 0.)

Emergency Legislation

For temporary (90 days) creation of this section, see § 1102 of Fiscal Year 2022 Budget Support Emergency Act of 2021 (D.C. Act 24-159, Aug. 23, 2021, 0 DCR 0).
