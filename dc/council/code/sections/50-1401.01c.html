Not Funded.


(Feb. 18, 2017, D.C. Law 21-208, § 4, 63 DCR 15285.)

Applicability

Applicability of D.C. Law 21-208: § 5 of D.C. Law 21-208 provided that the creation of this section by § 4 of D.C. Law 21-208 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been given effect.
