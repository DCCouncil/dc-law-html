§ 32–131.15. Report by the District of Columbia Auditor.

Recodified as § 32-531.15


(May 13, 2008, D.C. Law 17-152, § 16, 55 DCR 3452; Feb. 22, 2014, D.C. Law 20-89, § 2(i), 61 DCR 317; Aug. 17, 2016, D.C. Law 21-143, § 7, 63 DCR 7589.)

Effect of Amendments

The 2014 amendment by D.C. Law 20-89 deleted “with the posting requirements” following “businesses” in (1).

Applicability

Applicability of D.C. Law 21-143: § 14 of D.C. Law 21-143 provided that the change made to this section by § 7 of D.C. Law 21-143 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been given effect.

Section 7008 of D.C. Law 21-160 repealed § 14 of D.C. Law 21-143. Therefore the changes made to this section by D.C. Law 21-143 have been given effect.
