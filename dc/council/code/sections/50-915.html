§ 50–915. Mobile Customer Service Center Program. [Not Funded]

Not Funded.


(Jan. 25, 2018, D.C. Law 22-49, § 2, 64 DCR 12411.)

Applicability

Applicability of D.C. Law 22-49: § 3 of D.C. Law 22-49 provided that the creation of this section by § 2 of D.C. Law 22-49 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been implemented.
