§ 24–283. Uses of Inmate Welfare Fund.

The Fund shall be used for the following purposes, in order of priority:

(1) To stock the commissaries of District correctional facilities;

(2) To repay the initial appropriation used to finance the Fund; and

(3) To provide goods and services that benefit the general inmate population at District correctional facilities, as determined by the Inmate Welfare Fund Committee established in § 24-284.


(Mar. 2, 2007, D.C. Law 16-192, § 3004, 53 DCR 6899.)
