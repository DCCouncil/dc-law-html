§ 7–1811.02. Board of Trustees of the Tobacco Settlement Trust Fund. [Repealed]

 

Repealed.


(Oct. 20, 1999, D.C. Law 13-38, § 2302a; as added Oct. 19, 2000, D.C. Law 13-172, § 3721(b), 47 DCR 6308; June 5, 2003, D.C. Law 14-307, § 402(a), 49 DCR 11664; Nov. 13, 2003, D.C. Law 15-39, § 1504(a), 50 DCR 5668; Oct. 20, 2005, D.C. Law 16-33, § 1036, 52 DCR 7503; July 25, 2006, D.C. Law 16-142, § 2(b), 53 DCR 4412; May 2, 2015, D.C. Law 20-271, § 255(b), 62 DCR 1884.)

Section References

This section is referenced in § 7-1811.01 and § 7-1811.03.

Effect of Amendments

D.C. Law 15-39, in subsec. (e)(2), substituted “Fiscal Year 2004 shall not exceed $10,000.” for “fiscal year 2003 shall not exceed $1,000.”

D.C. Law 14-307 rewrote subsec. (e)(2); in subsec. (g), added par. (3); and rewrote subsec. (h)(2).

D.C. Law 16-33, in subsec. (d), substituted “Once funds are deposited into the Fund, the Board shall meet” for “The Board shall meet”.

D.C. Law 16-142, in subsec. (d), substituted “year” for “quarter”.
