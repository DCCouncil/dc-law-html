§ [28-3909.01]. Attorney General Authority.

Notwithstanding any District law, the Attorney General for the District of Columbia may use the enforcement authority set forth at [§ 28-3909] against any merchant, including a utility provider, that violates any provisions of this act (Act 23-328).


(June 8, 2020, D.C. Act 23-328, § 307(g), 67 DCR 7598.)
