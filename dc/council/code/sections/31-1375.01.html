§ 31–1375.01. Regulations.

 

The Commissioner may, in accordance with § 2-505, promulgate rules and regulations to carry out the purposes of this chapter.


(Apr. 11, 2003, D.C. Law 14-297, § 501, 50 DCR 330.)
