§ 1–201.01. Short title.

This chapter may be cited as the “District of Columbia Home Rule Act”.


(Dec. 24, 1973, 87 Stat. 777, Pub. L. 93-198, title I, § 101; Aug. 5, 1997, 111 Stat. 251, Pub. L. 105-33, title XI, § 11717(a).)
