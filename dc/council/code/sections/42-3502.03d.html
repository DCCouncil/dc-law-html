§ 42–3502.03d. Housing provider online filing and registration requirements.

(a) Beginning 180 days after October 30, 2018, and before December 13, 2019, a housing provider shall use the online provider portal developed pursuant to § 42-3502.03c(e-1) to file a registration statement or claim of exemption required by § 42-3502.05(f).

(b) Beginning December 13, 2019, a housing provider shall use the online provider portal created pursuant to § 42-3502.03c(b)(1) to file all documents and data required to be filed pursuant to this title and all regulations promulgated pursuant to this subchapter.


(July 17, 1985, D.C. Law 6-10, § 203b [203d]; as added Oct. 30, 2018, D.C. Law 22-168, § 2282(c), 65 DCR 9388.)
