§ 8–151.08e. Public reporting. [Not Funded]

Not Funded.


(Feb. 15, 2006, D.C. Law 16-51, § 108e; as added Apr. 7, 2017, D.C. Law 21-254, § 101(c), 64 DCR 2028.)

Applicability

Applicability of D.C. Law 21-254: § 501 of D.C. Law 21-254 provided that the creation of this section by § 101(c) of D.C. Law 21-254 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been given effect.
