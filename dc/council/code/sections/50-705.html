§ 50–705. Rules.

The Mayor shall, pursuant to subchapter 1 of Chapter 5 of Title 2, issue rules to implement the provisions of this chapter.


(Mar. 8, 1991, D.C. Law 8-243, § 6, 38 DCR 355; Mar. 17, 1994, D.C. Law 10-78, § 2(c), 40 DCR 8464; Mar. 14, 1995, D.C. Law 10-201, § 2(f), 41 DCR 7178.)
