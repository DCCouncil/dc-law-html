§ 21–1712. Short title.

This subchapter may be cited as the “Uniform Fiduciaries Act”.


(Sept. 14, 1965, 79 Stat. 778, Pub. L. 89-183, § 1; June 16, 1989, D.C. Law 8-9, § 2(c), 36 DCR 3361.)
