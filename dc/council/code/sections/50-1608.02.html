§ 50–1608.02. Proper bicycle equipment enforcement restrictions. [Not Funded]

Not Funded.


(Mar. 16, 1985, D.C. Law 5-179, § 9b; as added Dec. 23, 2020, D.C. Law 23-158, § 5(b), 67 DCR 13057.)

Applicability

Applicability of D.C. Law 23-158: § 14 of D.C. Law 23-158 provided that the creation of this section by § 5(b) of D.C. Law 23-158 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been implemented.
