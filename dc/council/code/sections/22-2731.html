§ 22–2731. Prostitution free zone; penalty. [Repealed]

 

Repealed.


(Apr. 24, 2007, D.C. Law 16-306, § 104, 53 DCR 8610; Dec. 10, 2009, D.C. Law 18-88, § 212, 56 DCR 7413; June 11, 2013, D.C. Law 19-317, § 206(c), 60 DCR 2064; May 6, 2015, D.C. Law 20-274, § 2, 61 DCR 12117.)

Effect of Amendments

D.C. Law 18-88, in subsec. (b)(1), substituted “480 consecutive hours” for “240 consecutive hours”.

The 2013 amendment by D.C. Law 19-317 substituted “not more than the amount set forth in § 22-3571.01” for “not more than $300” in (e).
