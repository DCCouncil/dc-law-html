§ 16–2343.02. Sanctions.

 

If any party refuses to submit to a test the party may be punished by contempt or by other sanctions that the court considers appropriate.


(Sept. 26, 1984, D.C. Law 5-123, § 3, 31 DCR 4056.)

Prior Codifications

1981 Ed., § 16-2343.2.

Editor's Notes

Section 28(c)(7) of D.C. Law 15-354 provided that the section designation of § 16-2343.2 of the District of Columbia Official Code is redesignated as § 16-2343.02.
