§ 38–2904. Weightings applied to counts of students enrolled at certain grade levels.

The student counts at certain grade levels and in certain programs shall be weighted to provide an amount per student differing from the basic foundation level in accordance with the following schedule:

Grade Level Weighting Per Pupil Allocation in [FY 2019]
Pre-Kindergarten 3 1.34 $14,713
Pre-Kindergarten 4 1.30 $14,273
Kindergarten 1.30 $14,273
Grades 1-5 1.00 $10,980
Grades 6-8 1.08 $11,858
Grades 9-12 1.22 $13,395
Alternative program 1.44 $15,810
Special education school 1.17 $12,846
Adult 0.89 $9,772
