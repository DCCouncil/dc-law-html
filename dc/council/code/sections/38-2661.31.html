§ 38–2661.31. Rules governing interscholastic athletics programs.

Within 120 days after the April 7, 2017, the Mayor, pursuant to subchapter I of Chapter 5 of Title 2, shall issue rules, consistent with this chapter, governing interscholastic athletics programs in the District.


(Apr. 7, 2017, D.C. Law 21-263, § 113, 64 DCR 2110.)

Applicability

Applicability of D.C. Law 21-263: § 301 of D.C. Law 21-263 provided that the creation of this section by § 113 of D.C. Law 21-263 is subject to the inclusion of the law’s fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan. Therefore that amendment has not been given effect.

Section 7033 of D.C. Law 22-33 repealed § 301 of D.C. Law 21-263. Therefore the changes made to this section by D.C. Law 21-263 have been given effect.
