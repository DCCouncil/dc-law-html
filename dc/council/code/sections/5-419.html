§ 5–419. Cadet program — Rules.

The Mayor or the Mayor’s designated agent may issue rules necessary for the implementation and operation of the cadet programs established pursuant to §§ 5-109.01 , 5-418, and 5-445.


(Mar. 9, 1983, D.C. Law 4-172, § 6, 29 DCR 5745; Oct. 8, 2016, D.C. Law 21-160, § 3063(b), 63 DCR 10775.)
