Editor's Notes

Mayor authorized to issue rules: Section 4 of D.C. Law 9-157 provided that the Mayor shall issue rules to implement the provisions of the act.

Uniform Law: This subchapter was based upon §§ 1 to 9 of the Uniform Anatomical Gift Act (1968 Act).

Delegation of Authority

Delegation of authority pursuant to Law 6-194, see Mayor’s Order 87-145, June 19, 1987.

Delegation of authority pursuant to D.C. Law 9-157, the “Tissue Transplantation Distribution Amendment Act of 1992”, see Mayor’s Order 93-61, May 12, 1993.
