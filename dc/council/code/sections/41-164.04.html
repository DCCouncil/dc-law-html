§ 41–164.04. Confidentiality agreement.

A person to be examined under section 7059 may require, as a condition of disclosure of the records of the person to be examined, that each person having access to the records disclosed in the examination execute and deliver to the person to be examined a confidentiality agreement that:

(1) Is in a form that is reasonably satisfactory to the Administrator; and

(2) Requires the person having access to the records to comply with the provisions of this part applicable to the person.


(Aug. 23, 2021, D.C. Act 24-159, § 7085, 68 DCR 008602.)
