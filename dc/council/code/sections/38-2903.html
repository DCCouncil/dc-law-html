§ 38–2903. Foundation level.

The foundation level or cost of providing public education services is $9,682 per student for Fiscal Year 2017 and subsequent fiscal years. The foundation level may be revised in subsequent years in accordance with provisions for inflation, revenue unavailability, and periodic review and revision of the Formula, pursuant to §§ 38-2909, 38-2910, and 38-2911.
