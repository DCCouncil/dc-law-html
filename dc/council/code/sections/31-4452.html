§ 31–4452. Date merger or consolidation completed.

 
The merger or consolidation shall be complete when the Mayor issues under § 31-4451(c)(3)(C) the certificate of merger or consolidation to the new or surviving company.
(June 19, 1934, ch. 672, ch. III, § 51; as added Mar. 14, 1985, D.C. Law 5-160, § 3(c), 32 DCR 39.)
