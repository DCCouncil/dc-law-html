§ 42–601. Deed, mortgage, and lease forms.

 

The following forms or forms to the like effect shall be sufficient, and any covenant, limitation, restriction, or proviso allowed by law may be added, annexed to, or introduced in the said forms. Any other form conforming to the rules herein laid down shall be sufficient:

Witness my hand and seal. _______________ [Seal.]

Witness our hands and seals. _______________ [Seal.]

_______________ [Seal.]

Witness my hand and seal. _______________ [Seal.]

Witness my hand and seal. _______________ [Seal.]

Witness my hand and seal. _______________ [Seal.]

Witness my hand and seal. _______________ [Seal.]

Given under my hand and seal. _______________ [Seal.]

Witness our hands and seals. _______________ [Seal.]

_______________ [Seal.]


(Mar. 3, 1901, 31 Stat. 1277, ch. 854, ch. 16, subch. 5; June 30, 1902, 32 Stat. 533, ch. 1329; Sept. 12, 2008, D.C. Law 17-231, § 33(b), 55 DCR 6758.)
