§ 47–3919. Rulemaking authority.

 

The Mayor shall issue rules to carry out the provisions of this act in accordance with § 2-505.


(Sept. 20, 1989, D.C. Law 8-26, § 23, 36 DCR 4723; enacted, Apr. 9, 1997, D.C. Law 11-254, § 2, 44 DCR 1575.)

Prior Codifications

1981 Ed., § 47-3919.

References in Text

“This act,” referred to in this section, is D.C. Law 8-26, which is primarily codified as § 47-3901 et seq.
