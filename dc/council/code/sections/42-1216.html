§ 42–1216. Office closed on Saturdays.

Notwithstanding the provisions of any other act, the Office of the Recorder of Deeds for the District of Columbia shall be closed on every Saturday.


(Aug. 2, 1946, 60 Stat. 860, ch. 758, § 1.)
